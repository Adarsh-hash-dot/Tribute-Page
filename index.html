Ludovico Einaudi

A Italian pianist and composer

Life of Ludovico Einaudi

  • 1955 - Born in Turin, Piedmont
  • 1965 - Started composing his own music as a teenager, first writing by playing a folk guitar
  • 1982 - Began his musical training at the Conservatorio Verdi in Milan, obtaining a diploma in composition.
  • 1982 - He took an orchestration class taught by Luciano Berio and was awarded a scholarship to the Tanglewood Music Festival.
  • 1988 - Album "Time Out" released
  • 1995 - Salgri released
  • 1999 - Best Music nominated for "Fuori Dal Mondo"
  • 2002 - Album "Alexandria" was released
  • 2004 - Album "Una mattina" was released
  • 2006 - Nominated for Best Technical Achievement for track "This Is England"
  • 2009 - Nightbook was released. The album saw Einaudi take a new direction with his music as he incorporated synthesized sounds alongside his piano.
  • 2013 - Einaudi's album In a Time Lapse
  • 2018 - Nominated for Outstanding Achievement in Music Award for "The Third Murder"
  • 2019 - Released Seven Days Walking: Day Seven
